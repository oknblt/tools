Bulut Projects
Collect · Merge · Edit · Compress PDFs — runs entirely in your browser

Collect PDF & Word files

Pick a main folder. Every PDF, DOC and DOCX inside its subfolders will be listed for review, then copied into a new Combined folder.

Your browser will ask permission to write files inside the folder you choose. Tip: in the picker, you can either click a folder once and press Select, or double-click to enter and then Select. Both work. Requires Chrome, Edge, or Opera.

Merge multiple PDFs into one

Pick the PDFs you want to combine — they'll be merged in the order shown.

Tip: select the PDFs from the Combined folder you generated in the Collect tab.

Edit a PDF

Rotate, delete, reorder pages. Add text or signatures. Redact sensitive areas.

Tap on the page to place
Redaction color:
Drag a rectangle on the page to redact.
Drag corners to resize · drag center to move · then apply.

Compress a PDF

Reduce PDF size by re-encoding embedded images at a lower resolution and quality. Text stays sharp; large photos get smaller.

Two strategies are tried: a lossless re-save (best for text-heavy documents) and a JPEG rasterization at the chosen DPI. Whichever produces the smaller file wins — and if neither beats the original, the original is kept and no download starts.
